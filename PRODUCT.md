# Product

## Register

brand

## Platform

web

## Users

Recruiters and hiring managers evaluating frontend engineering candidates. They arrive from a resume, LinkedIn, or a job application, usually mid-screening with many candidates open in tabs. They give the page seconds, not minutes; they are scanning for signal — is this person real, skilled, and worth a conversation? They are the audience the site speaks to; the person behind it is Pushpendra Singh, a frontend engineer with 4+ years specializing in Angular (v8–v19) and micro-frontend architecture.

## Product Purpose

A personal portfolio whose job is to make Pushpendra unforgettable in a crowded candidate pool. Success is a recruiter closing the tab thinking "that one stood out" — remembering the person, not just the stack — and downloading the resume to move him forward. Contact is the secondary path.

## Positioning

An engineer whose journey and personality come through in everything he builds. Personality and story are the differentiator; every section reinforces the human trajectory behind the work, not just the technology list.

## Conversion & proof

- Primary CTA: download the resume. Secondary: contact (form / email / LinkedIn).
- The line a visitor remembers after 10 seconds: "An engineer whose journey and personality come through in everything he builds."
- Belief ladder: (1) this person has a real story and voice, not a template; (2) the story is backed by real skill — 4+ years of Angular depth, v8→v19 migrations, micro-frontends, component libraries; (3) the skill produced measurable impact — 40% less manual regression testing via Playwright automation, 30% data-collection efficiency gains, 20% lower maintenance overhead; (4) I should download the resume before someone else grabs him.
- Proof on hand: real shipped projects with live links (AutoCrypto v3 — Angular/RxJS/Chart.js crypto dashboard at virtualsingh.github.io/autocrypto-v3; portfolio v3 at virtualsingh.github.io/portfolio-v3); work impact metrics from Aira Matrix and Vitrana (see belief ladder); two resumes with full detail at `public/Updated Resume Dec 2025.pdf` and `public/Pushpendra_Singh_Resume.pdf`.

## Brand Personality

Energy and ambition lead — a builder on a steep upward trajectory, forward-moving and bold. Confidence and trust, calm clarity, and moments of delight are the supporting notes: the energy never tips into noise, the credibility is carried by real work and real numbers, and playful touches appear as seasoning, not the meal.

## Anti-references

- Generic template/theme portfolios: Bootstrap/ThemeForest developer templates, stock card grids, interchangeable layouts.
- Flashy 3D/WebGL showcases: Awwwards-style effect-heavy sites where the spectacle buries the content.
- Corporate resume sites: dry LinkedIn-in-website-form pages with no voice or personality.
- Cluttered everything-sites: badge walls, skill percentage bars, twenty sections of noise.

## Design Principles

1. The story is the spine — journey and personality lead every section; the work is evidence inside the narrative, not a gallery beside it.
2. Momentum without noise — energy comes from pacing, motion, and typography, never from adding more sections or badges.
3. Real numbers, not adjectives — impact is proven with the actual metrics (40%, 30%, 20%), never "passionate" or "detail-oriented".
4. Effects serve the story — any visual flourish must make the narrative land harder; if it only decorates, it goes.
5. Respect the recruiter's clock — the 10-second scan must deliver the takeaway line; depth rewards those who stay.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Best-effort basics: sensible color contrast, full keyboard navigability, and reduced-motion alternatives for animations. No formal WCAG conformance target.
